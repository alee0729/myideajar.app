How it works

From scattered captures to structured thinking

IdeaJar is designed around one job: convert messy daily input into decisions you can act on.

1) Capture in any format

Voice notes, quick text, photos, and shared links/files all land in one unified inbox with timestamps and location context.

2) Understand automatically

IdeaJar classifies each capture (Idea, To-Do, Event, Email, Shopping, Note), adds summaries, and creates suggested next actions.

3) Surface patterns over time

Daily digests and semantic theme clustering connect related thoughts across days so you can notice opportunities sooner.

FAQ

Common questions before downloading

Does IdeaJar only work with voice notes?

No. You can capture through voice, text, photos, file attachments, and the iOS Share Extension from other apps.

Can I use IdeaJar when I'm offline?

Key flows include local fallback paths (like on-device transcription/classification) plus offline queue behavior for deferred cloud tasks.

What actions can IdeaJar suggest?

It can suggest reminders, calendar events, email drafts, map actions, links/calls/messages, clipboard actions, and research prompts.

Is this iPhone only?

Yes currently. IdeaJar is built for iPhone on iOS 17 and above.
